About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in Computer Science at Saint Louis University, specializing in event-based vision, artificial intelligence (AI), and robotics. My research focuses on developing innovative solutions in the realm of event-driven vision systems, particularly leveraging neuromorphic sensors to enhance real-time perception for robotics and autonomous systems. As a Graduate Research Assistant at SLU AIRLab, I work on cutting-edge projects that integrate event-based vision technologies with robotics to enable more efficient, adaptive, and intelligent systems.
